# 🛡️ BudgetVault

**Private budgeting that stays with you**

BudgetVault is a privacy-first budgeting app you can install on **iPhone, iPad, Android, tablets, laptops, and desktop**. It works offline, stores your data on your device, and protects saved data with strong local encryption.

---

## ✨ Why BudgetVault?

- 🔒 **Privacy-first** — your budget stays on your device
- 📶 **Offline-first** — works even without internet once installed
- 🧠 **Flexible planning** — weekly, bi-weekly, 4-weekly, and monthly budget cycles
- 📊 **Useful reports** — trends, category views, cycle comparisons, and custom range summaries
- 💾 **Secure backups** — export encrypted backups when you want extra peace of mind
- 📱 **Install like an app** — add it to your home screen on mobile or install it on desktop

## 🔐 Privacy & security

BudgetVault is built to be simple and private:

- 🔑 Your **password** protects your saved budget data
- 🔒 Optional **session PIN** can auto-lock the screen after inactivity
- 🧾 **Encrypted backup export** is the safest backup option
- 🚫 No accounts
- 🚫 No ads
- 🚫 No tracking built into the app

### Password vs PIN
- **Password** = encrypts and unlocks your saved data
- **PIN** = only locks the already-open app screen for the current session

If you lose your password, encrypted data cannot be recovered.

### Strong password tip
Use either:
- a **4-word passphrase**, or
- **16+ unique characters**

Do not reuse a password from email, banking, or social apps.

## 💾 Backups

### Recommended
- **Export Encrypted Backup** ✅

### Less safe
- **Export Plain JSON**
- **Export CSV**

Plain JSON and CSV files can be read by other apps or people if shared. Delete them when you no longer need them.
